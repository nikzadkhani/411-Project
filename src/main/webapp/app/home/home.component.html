Syncify is a web application that helps friends and families share their music using either Spotify or Apple Music. Find songs or create playlists that the user can share with a just a link!

We are an easy to use application and only have a few simple steps before you create your first playlist:

  1. Create your account
  2. Search for your music
  3. Create your own custom playlist
  4. Update your master playlist
  5. Share with others
